GCore v2 player — manual test harness

Standalone test of @gcorevideo/player outside Construct 3. See README.md. Open the console + Network tab. Enter a GCore embed URL or a direct master.m3u8, click Load, then pick a subtitle language.

Subtitles: (load a video first)

A3 – Volume units (are they 0..1 or 0..100?)

A4 – In-manifest CC tracks (no ?sub_lang= on the URL)

A5 – Low-latency / playback config

A6 – DVR window

A7 – Chrome plugins (BottomGear, Spinner, etc.)

A8 – Quality levels

D4 – External subtitle source (side-loaded VTT)
